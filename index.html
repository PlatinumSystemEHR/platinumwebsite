How does it work?


Check-In
Each patient receives a Chiropractic Health Card.They use it to register themselves electronically.They no longer have to sign a sign-in sheet.


Arrival List
The arrival list keeps the arrival sequence updated. Patients are seen according to their appointment time or their arrival time. Patients are called to the adjusting tables automatically by your recorded voice. The order in which patients are called can also be manually prioritized.


Calling System
The calling module manages patient flow and can be set to call the next patient to the adjusting table. Can be configured to call families or special need patients to specific tables.


Patient Chiropractic
Electronic Health File At the adjusting table, patients use their Chiropractic Health Card to access their Electronic Chiropractic Health File (Electronic Travel Card - ETC). A patient’s entire history, including everyday S.O.A.P. notes can be viewed from any computer in the clinic, where permitted. With a simple glance of the computer screen, you will have an excellent overview of the patient’s condition.


Soap Notes
You enter all SOAP notes using a Personalized Electronic Touch Pad. The configuration is set according to your needs. With the trainer, you will program your Electronic Touch Pad to complete SOAP notes such as: regular adjustment, listing, vertebrae, subjective and objective comments, exercises, therapies, etc. At the touch of a button, you enter complete SOAP notes, release the table and prepare the system to call the next patient. With data entry this fast, you gain patient adjusting time, and drastically improve your note taking ability!


Check-Out
Patients are automatically billed for the day’s services, without any intervention from you or your CA. When needed, you can easily send messages to the Front Desk, directly through the system! If your patients have taken multiple appointments and have pre-paid their Financial Care Plan, they can leave the clinic without even stopping at the Front Desk! Your patients expect an easy, efficient, professional experience.
