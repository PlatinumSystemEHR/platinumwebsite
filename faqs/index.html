QUESTIONS/ANSWERS

PLATINUM OUTSOURCE BILLING SERVICE

Q: Do I need to use Platinum System in my clinic to get the Platinum Outsource Billing Service?

A: Yes, you need to use Platinum System because the Platinum Outsource Billing Service works with the SMART Insurance Management software created by Platinum System and it is linked with client’s Platinum System.

Q: .Can I use my own clearing house and get the Platinum Outsource Billing Service?

A: No, you absolutely need to use Gateway EDI/TriZetto as your clearing house if you want to use our Platinum Outsource Billing Service unless you absolutely need to use another Clearing House for specific payers. Platinum System has created a great business relationship with Gateway EDI/TriZetto and we work closely to make sure our clients get paid the most efficient manner.

Q: .Do I have to pay the clearing house Gateway EDI/TriZetto fees?

A: If your primary residence for tax purposes is anywhere but the US, your country determines the minimum income or asset requirement that allows you to participate in venture capital investments. The sign-up process for our investment options is, therefore, country-specific; you check off the requirements that are satisfied and, when necessary, specify the reason that you meet each requirement.

Q: .What is Gateway EDI/TriZetto monthly fee?

A: Pricing between $70 and $125 (A la Carte; Essential and SMART packages). Call them at 800-969-3666 to find out which package will suit your needs the best..

Q: .What is the rate you charge?

A: There are three billing options to choose from:

  • Option 6%: - First three months: 6% of paid claims or minimum of $250 per month

    1. Following months: 6% of paid claims or minimum of $299 per month
    2. Clinic handles all P.I. and non-commercial paper claims
    3. The Doctor is responsible for Gateway EDI/TriZetto fee. Pricing between $70 and $125 (à la Carte; Essential and SMART packages)
  • Option 7%: - First three months: 7% of paid claims or minimum of $250 per month
    1. Following months: 7% of paid claims or minimum of $299 per month
    2. All paper claims are included
    3. The Doctor is responsible for Gateway EDI/TriZetto fee. Pricing between $70 and $125 (à la Carte; Essential and SMART packages)
  • Option 8%: - First three months: 8% of paid claims or minimum of $250 per month
    1. Following months: 8% of paid claims or minimum of $399
    2. Additional doctors: add $100 per Doctor per month
    3. All paper claims are included
    4. Platinum pays Gateway EDI/TriZetto fees

Q: .What do you mean by PAID claims?

A: We mean the collected amount.
